Burger

Burger is the king of junk food and comfort food.Lets dive into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.

  4. Step 3

    5. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.

  5. Step 4

    6. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

  6. Step 5

    7. Serve hot and enjoy!
